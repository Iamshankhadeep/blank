## ðŸ”¥ PRODUCTIVITY HACKS

### Morning Routine is Your Competitive Advantage
- **5:30 AM wake = 3.5 hours** before most people start
- This is when you do your best work
- **Protect this time fiercely** - no meetings, no Slack

### The Power of Templates
- Duplicate daily/weekly templates (don't start from scratch)
- Templates = consistency
- Consistency = results

### No Zero Days
- Even if you're tired, do the **3-3-3 minimum**

- Recordings: Record multiple if you're in the flow

- Podcast while showering/commuting
- Read articles during lunch
- Listen to courses while exercising

### Weekly Reviews are Critical
- **Every Sunday evening:** Review + plan
- Track what's working
- Adjust what's not
- Celebrate wins
