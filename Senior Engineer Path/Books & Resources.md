## ðŸ“– Reading Strategy

### The 3-Pass Method

**Pass 1: Skim (30 min)**
- Read chapter intro and summary
- Look at headings and examples
- Decide if relevant

**Pass 2: Read (1-2 hours)**
- Read carefully
- Take notes
- Skip proofs/deep technical details

**Pass 3: Practice (varies)**
- Do exercises
- Apply to your code
- Teach someone else
