# Weekly Practice Tracker

Track your progress and build senior engineer skills through deliberate practice. Copy this template each week.

## How to Use This

1. **Each Monday:** Copy this template to a new note: "Week of [Date]"
2. **Daily:** Check off items as you complete them
3. **Friday:** Reflect on what you learned
4. **Repeat:** Consistency beats intensity

### Quality Over Quantity
- Better to review 1 PR thoroughly than 5 rushed
- Better to refactor 1 function well than touch 10 poorly
- Better to understand 1 concept deeply than skim 10

### Apply Immediately
- Don't just read - apply
- Don't just learn - teach
- Don't just know - do

### Track Progress
- Small wins compound
- Look back monthly to see growth
- Celebrate progress
- Adjust as needed

### Don't Be Perfect
- Missing a day is okay
- Some weeks are harder than others
- Progress, not perfection
- Keep going
